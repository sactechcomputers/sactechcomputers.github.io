About Us

The Sir Ahmadu Bello Memorial Foundation (SABMF) was founded by the Governors of the 19 northern states of Nigeria in cooperation with the associates, friends, admirers and well-wishers of the late Sir Ahmadu Bello, KBE, the Sardauna of Sokoto and Premier of the defunct northern region.

Northern Education Review Summit.
Critical stakeholders from the 19 northern states and FCT to reviewed the situation of education in the region. It examined the successes and challenges of education and proffered far reaching measures to improve the situation based on the existing plans.

Sir Ahmadu Bello Distinguished Service Awards.
In recognition of the selfless and exemplary service of individuals to the nation, the Foundation has instituted the annual Sir Ahmadu Bello Memorial Distinguished Service Awards, whereby deserving individuals are selected in an open and transparent manner.
